About Us

Established in 2018, Genesis Media is run by group of photographers & business developers with one foot in media and one foot in academia, strive to help make memories & focus on delivering quality content. We believe media content is more than just that, it is a voice to your emotions, memories, vision & growth. From fun and frolic at a wedding event to the intricate values of your company being showcased, we do the best to translate them into pixels.

Our mission is to create lasting memories with passion and enthusiasm whilst exceed your expectations at every level.

Why choose us?


We are a client and team centric firm; we believe our ultimate USP is our team members and our impeccable sense of clientele service.

Our team members are creative & certified in the field of media production. We also understand the need of emerging technicalities & storytelling while creating content for your occasions and your branding needs.

Partner with us to develop captivating, evidence-based stories for mass audiences.
